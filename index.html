India ranks 111th out of 125 countries on the Global Hunger Index and reports from the United Nations suggest that there are nearly 195 Million Undernourished people in India that make up a quarter of the world's undernourished population.

We here at Khao India have taken the initiative to solve the hunger crisis in India by creating a system that tackles two problems at once, i.e, ensuring food security to Indian citizens and stopping food wastage. We aim to fulfil these goals by fostering relationships between Restaurants/Catering Servies and NGOs so that any food that would have otherwise gone to waste at the end of the day, now gets distributed to people in dire need of food. We invite restaurants, caterers and NGOs to join our platform.
